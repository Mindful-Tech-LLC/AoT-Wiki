Founded in 2011, the Attack on Titan Wiki started as a fan wiki hosted on the Fandom network and centered around the Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人, Shingeki no Kyojin) series by Hajime Isayama, and later expanded to several social media platforms.

Our wiki serves as a detailed encyclopedia that provides the most up-to-date, thorough, and accurate information about the AoT franchise, with strict quality standards to guarantee that only official and confirmed information makes it to our articles.

Our social media presence, which is currently spread across 4 social networks (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and Discord), is the main source of AoT news on the web, and it's also the host of almost 3 million AoT fans; being by far the largest and most active community about the series on the Internet, and one of the largest manga/anime communities overall.

On our websites, you'll find well-moderated places to discuss everything AoT with many more fans like you.
